## Quick Summary

**The 3-second rule:**  
If you receive a link asking you to log in — STOP. Type `instagram.com` manually into your browser.

---

## Why This Matters

In 2024, over **1.5 million Instagram accounts** were compromised by phishing attacks. Most victims were regular people, not celebrities.

This guide teaches you to recognize the signs **before** you get hacked.
