# 🔍 How to Spot a Fake Instagram Login Page

**A complete guide for non-technical users.**

---

## The 30-Second Version

**If you receive a link asking you to log into Instagram:**

1. **STOP** — Don't click it yet
2. **LOOK** at the web address — is it exactly `instagram.com`?
3. **THINK** — Did you expect this message?
4. **GO** — Type `instagram.com` manually into your browser

---

## 7 Red Flags to Watch For

### 1️⃣ The URL is wrong
- ✅ **Real:** `instagram.com` or `www.instagram.com`
- ❌ **Fake:** `instagram-login.xyz`, `instagr4m.com`, `instagram-security-verify.com`

**How to check:** Look at the address bar in your browser.

---

### 2️⃣ The logo looks off
- ✅ **Real:** Crisp, clean, uses the "Billabong" font
- ❌ **Fake:** Blurry, pixelated, or uses a generic font like Arial

**How to check:** Compare it to the real Instagram logo you know.

---

### 3️⃣ The password "Show" button doesn't work
- ✅ **Real:** Tapping "Show" reveals your password
- ❌ **Fake:** Button does nothing or is missing entirely

**How to check:** Tap the "Show" button. If nothing changes — it's fake.

---

### 4️⃣ The "Forgot password?" link goes to a strange place
- ✅ **Real:** Goes to `instagram.com/accounts/password/reset/`
- ❌ **Fake:** Goes nowhere (`#`) or to a different domain

**How to check:** Tap and hold the link to see where it really goes.

---

### 5️⃣ The message asking you to log in is suspicious

**Common fake messages:**
- ❌ "Your account will be deleted if you don't verify"
- ❌ "Someone tried to log into your account"
- ❌ "You've won a verification badge"
- ❌ "We noticed suspicious activity"

**How to check:** Did you receive this out of nowhere? Does it try to scare you? That's a scam.

---

### 6️⃣ It accepts any password
- ✅ **Real:** Says "Sorry, your password was incorrect"
- ❌ **Fake:** Accepts anything you type and moves on

**How to check:** Type a fake password like "test123". If it doesn't give an error — it's fake.

---

### 7️⃣ You clicked a link instead of typing it yourself
- ✅ **Safe:** You typed `instagram.com` manually
- ❌ **Risky:** You clicked a link from a text, email, or DM

**How to check:** Think back — did you type the address or click it?

---

## 🚨 What To Do If You Already Entered Your Password

**Don't panic. Do these immediately:**

1. **Change your password** on the real Instagram app
2. **Enable 2-Factor Authentication** (2FA) — see our guide
3. **Check your recent login activity** in Instagram settings
4. **Log out of all devices** (Instagram settings → Security → Log out of all devices)
5. **Check for any suspicious messages or posts** sent from your account
